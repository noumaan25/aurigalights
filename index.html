Our Store

Established in 1999, Auriga Lights has been a trusted name in premium chandeliers and decorative lighting. Located at a prime spot in the bustling Troop Bazar, Hyderabad, our store has been illuminating homes, hotels, and businesses with elegance and brilliance for over two decades. We offer a curated collection of classic, contemporary, and custom-designed chandeliers that blend craftsmanship with style. Whether you're looking to enhance your living space or make a bold statement, Auriga Lights is your destination for quality and sophistication in lighting.
